FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about JustLend Swap on TRON.

What is JustLend Swap?
+
JustLend Swap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) and lending protocol built on the TRON blockchain. It enables users to swap TRC20 tokens, supply assets to earn yield, borrow against collateral, provide liquidity, and stake JST tokens — all without a central intermediary.
How do I start using JustLend Swap?
+
Simply install a TRON-compatible wallet such as TronLink or TokenPocket, fund it with TRX or any TRC20 token, then visit just-lend-swap.vercel.app and click "Launch App." Connect your wallet and you can immediately start swapping, lending, or staking.
What wallets are supported?
+
JustLend Swap supports all major TRON-compatible wallets including TronLink (browser extension and mobile), TokenPocket, Trust Wallet, imToken, BitKeep, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets. We recommend TronLink for the best experience.
What are the trading fees on JustLend Swap?
+
The standard swap fee is 0.3% per trade, of which 0.25% goes directly to liquidity providers and 0.05% goes to the protocol treasury. Stable pool swaps have a reduced fee of 0.05%. TRON network fees (Energy/Bandwidth) are minimal, typically less than $0.01 per transaction.
What is JST token and what is it used for?
+
JST (JUST) is the native governance and utility token of the JustLend ecosystem on TRON. It is used for protocol governance voting, staking to earn protocol revenue, boosting liquidity mining rewards, and accessing premium features. JST stakers receive veJST which represents their voting power.
How does the staking system work?
+
Users lock JST tokens for a chosen duration (30, 90, 180, or 365 days). Longer locks receive a higher multiplier — up to 3x for a 365-day lock. Rewards are distributed every 8-hour epoch and can be claimed or auto-compounded. The base APY is 6.1%, scaling up to 18.4% at maximum lock.
Can I unstake JST before the lock period ends?
+
Yes, early unstaking is possible but incurs a 10% penalty on the principal amount. This penalty is redistributed to remaining stakers. Accrued rewards earned up to the point of unstaking are still claimable. We recommend waiting until lock expiry to avoid the penalty.
How does lending and borrowing work?
+
Lenders supply supported assets (TRX, USDT, JST, USDD, etc.) to protocol pools and earn variable interest determined by pool utilization. Borrowers provide collateral to borrow assets with a loan-to-value ratio. All positions are over-collateralized to ensure protocol solvency. Liquidation occurs automatically if collateral ratio drops below the threshold.
Is JustLend Swap audited and secure?
+
The protocol smart contracts have been audited by leading blockchain security firms including SlowMist and Certik. All contracts are fully open-source and verifiable on TRONSCAN. A bug bounty program is active with rewards up to $500,000 for critical vulnerabilities. However, DeFi always carries inherent smart contract risk.
How do liquidity pools work on JustLend Swap?
+
Liquidity providers deposit token pairs into pools and receive LP tokens representing their share. When traders swap through that pool, the 0.25% LP fee is distributed proportionally. LP positions also qualify for JST liquidity mining incentives, which can be boosted up to 3x by staking JST. Concentrated liquidity positions offer higher capital efficiency.
What is impermanent loss and how can I minimize it?
+
Impermanent loss occurs when the price ratio of tokens in a liquidity pool changes relative to when they were deposited. To minimize it, you can provide liquidity to stable pools (e.g., USDT/USDD) where assets are pegged, choose pools with high trading volume to offset losses with fees, or use concentrated liquidity ranges strategically.
What is USDD and can I use it on JustLend Swap?
+
USDD is TRON's decentralized over-collateralized stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar. It is fully supported on JustLend Swap for swapping, lending (with one of the highest supply APYs), and as a stable base for liquidity pools. Holding USDD lets you earn yield while maintaining a stable USD-denominated position.
How are interest rates determined on the lending protocol?
+
Interest rates are algorithmically set based on pool utilization rate — the percentage of supplied assets currently borrowed. As utilization increases, borrow rates rise to attract more supply and manage risk. The rate model features a kink point at 80% utilization where rates accelerate sharply to maintain liquidity buffers.
What happens if my collateral is liquidated?
+
If your health factor drops below 1.0 (collateral value falls below the liquidation threshold), liquidators can repay up to 50% of your debt and receive your collateral at a 5–10% discount. The remaining collateral and debt are returned to you. Always maintain a health factor above 1.5 to avoid liquidation risk during market volatility.
Does JustLend Swap support cross-chain bridging?
+
JustLend Swap natively operates on the TRON blockchain. For cross-chain assets, we integrate with TRON's official bridge for ETH/BSC/Polygon assets and support cross-chain USDT (which is the most traded stablecoin on TRON). Third-party bridges like Multichain are also compatible with the protocol.
How do I participate in governance?
+
Stake JST tokens to receive veJST (vote-escrowed JST). Your veJST balance equals your voting power. Governance proposals include protocol upgrades, fee adjustments, new pool listings, treasury spending, and risk parameter changes. Voting happens on-chain via the governance portal, with a 7-day voting window per proposal.
Why build on TRON instead of Ethereum?
+
TRON offers transaction finality in under 3 seconds, fees below $0.01, and handles over 2,000 TPS. Ethereum gas fees often exceed $5–50 per transaction, making small-value DeFi uneconomical. TRON also hosts the largest USDT liquidity of any blockchain, making it ideal for stablecoin-based DeFi strategies.
Are there any KYC or identity requirements?
+
No. JustLend Swap is a fully permissionless, non-custodial DeFi protocol. There is no KYC, account registration, or identity verification required. All you need is a compatible TRON wallet. You retain full custody of your funds at all times — the protocol never holds your private keys.
How do I track my portfolio and earnings?
+
The JustLend Swap dashboard provides a real-time overview of all your positions: supplied assets, borrowed amounts, LP positions, staking balances, and accumulated rewards. You can also track all transactions on TRONSCAN using your wallet address. Portfolio analytics including PnL, yield history, and ROI are available in the app.
What is the maximum leverage available for borrowing?
+
The maximum loan-to-value (LTV) ratio varies by asset — for example, TRX has a max LTV of 65%, while USDT can be collateralized at 80%. Effective leverage is therefore capped at approximately 2.5–5x depending on assets used. Loop strategies (supply → borrow → supply) can further increase exposure but significantly raise liquidation risk.
